# Canonical Publication Procedure

1. Preserve the Gate-1-approved `spec.md` product body as the semantic baseline.
2. Validate the five `task-slices/*.md` files against `tasks.md`, approved contracts, and traceability.
3. Perform one controlled publication to `homework-3/specification.md`: product body first, then a clearly delimited low-level-task section.
4. Confirm product-body semantic equivalence independently from low-level-task parity.
5. Treat `homework-3/specification.md` as canonical only after both checks pass.
6. Any product behavior change must first update and reapprove `spec.md`; task-only corrections must remain synchronized with `tasks.md` and be reanalyzed.

The physical file becomes canonical at step 5, never during individual story drafting.
